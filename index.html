Deimos

Deimos is the smaller and outer of the two natural satellites of the planet Mars. With a mean radius of 6.2 km it takes 30.3 hours to orbit Mars.

Discovered by:
Asaph Hall
Discovery date:
August 17, 1877
Adjectives:
Deimosian
